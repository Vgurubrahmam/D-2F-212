TECH DRAMA

About the tech drama

A tech drama event is a bit like a mix of technology and storytelling. It's where people use technology to create and tell stories in exciting and creative ways. Imagine actors using fancy gadgets, computer graphics, or even virtual reality to bring a story to life on stage or screen. It's like a fusion of theater and technology. These events can be really captivating and immersive, giving the audience a unique and entertaining experience. So, in simple words, a tech drama event is all about using cool tech stuff to tell engaging and often futuristic stories. It's where the world of technology meets the world of drama and storytelling.

THUG-OF-WAR

About the thug of war

Tug of war is like a friendly battle of strength between two teams. Each team stands on opposite sides of a rope, and they try to pull the rope in their direction. The team that manages to pull the rope past a certain point or make the other team lose their balance wins. It's a simple yet fun game that tests who's stronger and can work together better as a team..

TREASURE HUNT

About the treasure hunt

A treasure hunt is like a real-life adventure game where you follow clues or a map to find hidden treasures or objects. It's a bit like a quest or a puzzle. The clues lead you from one place to another until you finally discover the hidden "treasure," which could be a prize, a message, or just something fun to find. It's a great way to have fun and explore while solving riddles or challenges along the way.
